Meeting our economic and climate challenges, head-on.

Vote March 5th for Dan Jones to be Mayor of Montpelier.

Electing Dan means directing city administrative staff to find and obtain state and federal support necessary to:

  1. Fix our maintenance-delayed infrastructure, and
  2. Mitigate and respond effectively to new-normal extreme weather events

Dan Jones aims to prioritize long-term planning while addressing immediate needs, advocates for stronger collaboration between the mayor, city council, and administrative staff, and insists on “no budget without measurable objectives”.

Request a yard sign to show your support, or read Dan’s priorities and the full platform to learn more.

Our citizens deserve to know how our taxes and resources are going to be used to preserve the city’s core functions and prepare a resilient response to the coming predictable challenges.

Dan has spent years developing the requisite knowledge of Montpelier’s municipal charter and procedures, building trust and relationships with city council members, and demonstrating his commitment to helping our city meet its growing challenges.

Montpelier cannot wait. It is time to do the hard things.
